Birthday party Ideas

Age Birthday Party Ideas
1-5 Invite some family and friends and make a small party at home. Serve lots of cake and take lots of pictures to document the growth of your baby. For the really little kids, a cake smash is a cute idea that will result in lots of laughter and adorable pictures.
6-10 Invite some friends of your child and make a themed party. The theme can be something your child loves or whatever you find in the Dollar Tree or Party City. Some cute and easy themes you can do are Disney, cars, circus, animals, and mikey mouse. You can also play party games like pin the tail on the donkey. And of course don't forget the birthday cake!
10-15 Time to do something a little more. Go somewhere fun, either as a family or with friends. You can go hiking or have a picnic in the park. Or you can take your child ice skating, rock climbing, or to a trampoline park like Sky Zone.
15-20 Make a surprise party! It can be in your house or in the pizza or ice cream shop. The main thing is that your child should be surprised. Turn off the lights and see how high you can get your child to jump when you yell surprise.
